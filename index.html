    About Me

    I'm a senior researcher in the Algorithms group at Microsoft Research Redmond.

    I completed my PhD (dissertation) in the theory group at the University of Washington Allen School of CSE, where I had the honor to have been advised by Thomas Rothvoss, and spent one year as a postdoc in the CSDM group at the Institute for Advanced Study.

    I coached ICPC teams at Cornell from 2016 to 2018, and at UW from 2019 to 2023.

    I am broadly interested in designing algorithms for problems in combinatorial optimization, using tools from probability, convex geometry, and discrepancy theory.
